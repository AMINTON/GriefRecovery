Rediscover the ability within yourself to transform the quality of your life.

The Grief Recovery Method® is an evidence-based, step-by-step course on how to deal with the grief stemming from a loss.

Time Doesn't Heal.

Taking action does. Whether it's been days or decades since your loss, your heart can still feel empty. Grief persists even when we try our hardest to pull through everyday routines acting as if we're okay. It can affect our ability to feel, focus, react and overall be present-in work, relationships, school and more. It's never too late to take action toward your grief recovery. The Grief Recovery Method® is an action program consisting of weekly meetings and readings from the Grief Recovery Handbook® where you will uncover the information and tools necessary to begin moving forward in your life. Allison is a Certified Grief Recovery Specialist® who can help you navigate the feelings and grief that arise after unfortunate loss so you can finally heal your broken heart and rediscover your ability to enjoy life once again.

Any Change Can Trigger Grief
Positive and negative changes and milestones including marriage, a new school, parenthood and moving can trigger grief.
Time Doesn't Heal
Do you find yourself crying at the thought of someone you lost months, years or even decades ago?
Pet Loss
Pets live alongside us experiencing much of our day-to-day lifestyle. The sudden absence of a close companion, though not human, can certainly cause substantial grief, even years past.
Missed Opportunities
Experiencing an abortion, miscarriage, infertility, or adoption can yield significant grief from the unfulfilled relationships or milestones that you had hoped to achieve in your life.
Feelings of Guilt
Grief is heavily stereotyped but can be completely numbing or unemotional. Learn to decipher and navigate these feelings guilt-free.
Helping Children With Loss
Children experience grief from life events including divorce, moving, changing schools and more. Learn how to support your child appropriately in a four week action program.
